How this works (read me). $TOMB is a plain ERC-20 on Robinhood Chain (fixed supply, no mint function) — so "expansion" here is paid as emissions from a finite reserve, not real minting. Staking, bonding and the treasury run on an off-chain ledger; rewards settle by scripted airdrop. The TWAP/peg shown is simulated for the app until a live pool oracle is wired. Algorithmic peg models are high-risk and most have failed — only stake what you can lose. Trustless on-chain settlement (audited contracts) is the planned upgrade.
